Regular flying drones might be annoying sometimes, unsafe and even invaders of our privacy. What about a drone you can climb into and fly to the grocery store?

This is what the PassengerDrone company, based in Switzerland going to offer us, with their new two-seater manned aircraft. It flies without its passengers having to do anything beside interact with a touchscreen.

But however. Living in the flying future won't be cheap, PassengerDrone have estimated that it will cost between $150,000 and $200,000.
